Welcome I'm Kiara Castillo Magallanes

Welcome to my photography website! I’m a student at Indiana University’s Luddy School, currently exploring the world of photography through my coursework at Herron School of Art and Design . This website is a space where I share some of the images I’ve captured moments that have caught my eye, stories told through light and composition, and personal experiments as I develop my skills behind the lens.

Photography, for me, is more than just taking pictures; it’s about observing the world differently, noticing the small details, and capturing emotions that might otherwise go unnoticed. Through my classes, I’ve been learning about technique, composition, and the power of visual storytelling.
